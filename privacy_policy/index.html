Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Thank you for using the UC Merced Hub ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (the "Service").

Important: This Service is a student-led project and is not officially affiliated with the University of California, Merced.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in privacy by design. We have minimized data collection to the absolute essentials required for the Service to function.

A. Personal Data

We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide your name, email address, or phone number to use the primary features of the app (viewing the map and events).

B. Usage and Device Data

When you access the Service, we may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device type and operating system to help us debug crashes and improve performance.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous, aggregate data regarding which features of the app are used most frequently (e.g., number of unique visitors).

C. Location Information

To provide navigation and routing features (e.g., drawing a path from your current location to a building), our app may request access to your device's location services.

  • This location data is processed locally on your device or sent ephemerally to our routing engine to calculate distance and direction.
  • We do not store your movement history or real-time location on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service (e.g., displaying the campus map).
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • To monitor the usage of the Service to detect, prevent, and address technical issues.

3. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies and services to facilitate our Service ("Service Providers"), such as hosting providers or map data providers.

  • Map Data: Our maps may utilize data from OpenStreetMap or other open-source mapping contributors.
  • Hosting: Our backend services are hosted on secure cloud infrastructure (e.g., Render).

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. While no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information. Since we do not collect sensitive personal identity information, your risk is minimal.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By joining our Discord Server
  • By visiting our support page: Support Page
