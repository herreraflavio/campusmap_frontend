About this site

This page is an unofficial, map-based view of UC Merced Daily Crime & Fire Log entries. It is intended to make it easier to browse what has been reported and posted, at a glance.

  • Focused on crime and fire-log entries (not events).
  • Designed to update as new daily logs are posted by UC Merced.
  • For authoritative details, always refer to the official sources below.

Source

Primary source: UC Merced Police Department Daily Crime & Fire Log page.

  • UC Merced Police — Daily Crime & Fire Log

The Daily Crime & Fire Log is maintained under Clery Act-related reporting and includes reported incidents and fires in student housing, as applicable.
