About this site

This is a hub for UC Merced events. It's not an official university site. Our goal is to make it simple to see what's going on around campus at a glance.

  • Mobile-first, fast, and accessible.
  • Map view shows every event from Presence.
  • Open to all student orgs, departments, and teams.
