Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an official UC Merced app? ‚ñº
No. The UC Merced Events Map is a student-led project designed to help the campus community. We are not officially affiliated with the University of California, Merced administration.
How do I get my club's event on the map? ‚ñº
Currently, we aggregate public events from various campus sources. If you want to ensure your club's event is featured, please join our Discord server and post in the requests channel, or DM us on Instagram.
I found a bug or a map error. ‚ñº
We appreciate your help! The fastest way to report a bug is via our Discord server in the #bug-reports channel. You can also message us on X (Twitter) or Instagram.
Is there a mobile app available? ‚ñº
Yes! We are currently working on an iOS application that will be available on the App Store soon. It will feature the interactive events map and walking directions.
